📉 Multidimensional Poverty Index

Non-monetary poverty measured across 4 dimensions: living conditions, education, health & employment

MPI — PAIReD District Overview
The Multidimensional Poverty Index (MPI) captures deprivations across multiple dimensions beyond income. Districts are assessed on living conditions, access to education, health outcomes, and employment quality. Lower scores reflect greater multidimensional deprivation.
